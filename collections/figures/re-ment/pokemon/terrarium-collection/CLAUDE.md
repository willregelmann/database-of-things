# Terrarium Collection — curation hints

## Sub-lines not yet curated

Re-Ment has released several variants under the "Terrarium Collection"
banner beyond the mainline numbered volumes curated here (`vol-01`
through `vol-15`):

- **EX regional volumes** — e.g. "テラリウムコレクションEX アローラ地方編"
  (Alola region), "...ガラル地方編" (Galar region, two waves), "...駆け巡る
  パルデアの世界へ" (Paldea) — regional-Pokémon-themed side releases, not
  part of the mainline numbering.
- **DX** — "テラリウムコレクションDX ひだまりの森のピカチュウ" (2018-12-10),
  a single deluxe release.
- **Unnumbered named specials** — e.g. "Four Seasons" (2020-04-24), "めぐる
  季節の中で" (2021-06-26), "Happyなまいにち" (2023-03-20), "のんびりな
  ひととき" (2024-11-25).

Whether these belong nested under `terrarium-collection/` (as e.g.
`ex-alola/`, `dx/`) or as sibling series under `pokemon/` is an open
question — decide it deliberately when curating one, rather than picking a
placement to unblock a single PR.

## Base/display style changed over time

Volumes 1-9 use the original clear PokéBall terrarium alone. Volume 10
introduced a display pedestal (a "gumball machine"-style stand); Volume 15
uses a distinct silver/white pedestal calling out the Pokémon franchise's
30th anniversary. This is packaging/presentation, not modeled as a separate
attribute — noted here only so a future curator isn't surprised by the
photos looking different across volumes.

## Verified via official product photography

Every figure currently filed here was identified by downloading and
visually inspecting Re-Ment's own gallery photos for that volume's product
page (`re-ment.co.jp/product/r<NNNNN>`), cross-referenced against a
retailer/fan checklist for the initial candidate roster — not from
checklist text alone. See [`../../CLAUDE.md`](../../CLAUDE.md) for why: both
gallery photo order and Japanese→English name translation are unreliable
enough on their own to cause real misidentifications (several caught and
corrected during initial curation).
